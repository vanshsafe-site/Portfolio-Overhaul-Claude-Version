About Me

Builder • Prompt Engineer • Diploma CSE Student

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Vansh Garg, a Computer Science & Engineering Diploma student passionate about software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and prompt engineering.

I graduated from St. Joseph's College, Prayagraj under the ICSE Board with 94% in Class 10.

Since then, I have been actively learning through real projects, experimentation, and practical implementation of modern technologies.

My Philosophy

I love vibe coding, but I strongly believe that the creator should remain involved in every major decision.

Whether it is designing features, planning architecture, deciding user experience, or solving technical problems, I prefer understanding the system rather than blindly generating code.

Technology should be learned through experimentation, building, breaking things, fixing them, and understanding why they work.

My View On AI

I use Generative AI as a learning accelerator, mentor, and brainstorming partner.

I discourage complete dependency on agentic AI from a learning perspective.

Instead, I encourage students to:

  • Generate code
  • Read and understand it
  • Modify it
  • Combine multiple approaches
  • Explore hosting and backend systems
  • Learn through real projects

Prompt Engineering

One of my strongest skills is Prompt Engineering.

I enjoy creating structured prompts that help AI understand requirements more effectively.

Instead of asking:

How to Build an AI?

I prefer asking:

Create a chatbot in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that accepts user input, checks for specific keywords, and returns suitable responses.

My goal is to transform vague requests into detailed instructions that produce higher quality results.
